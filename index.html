Eating Disorders

Behavioral conditions characterized by severe and persistent disturbance in eating behaviors and associated distressing thoughts and emotions. They can be very serious conditions affecting physical, psychological and social function.

Learn More

Eating disorders are common and increasing in prevalence.
There is a lifetime estimated prevalence of 8.4% in women and 2.2% for men.


Suicide is up to 31 times more likely to occur for someone with anorexia nervosa
and 7.5 times higher for someone with bulimia nervosa than the general population.
